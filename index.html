About

Each journey is an adventure and all adventures need exciting travel partners. TravelPaddi is a web app that helps you connect with other travellers with the same destination and similar time frames. It is a great way to meet and connect with new people especially when travelling alone. I have always loved connecting with people especially when i travel to a new city. I believe this app will help people like me.
